Privacy Policy for Hiifi Messenger

1. Introduction

Welcome to Hiifi Messenger! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application. By using Hiifi Messenger, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

When you use Hiifi Messenger, we may collect the following types of data:

Personal Information:
  • Email address, phone number, or social login credentials used during account creation (via Firebase Authentication).
  • Profile details such as name and profile picture, if added by the user.
Messaging Data:
  • Text messages.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage user accounts.
  • To enable secure messaging and real-time communication.
  • To deliver notifications and updates relevant to your messages or activity.
  • To improve app functionality and user experience.
  • To troubleshoot bugs and enhance security.

4. Data Protection and Security

  • All data is transmitted using industry-standard encryption (HTTPS).
  • User authentication is handled securely using Firebase Authentication.
  • Stored media and messages are protected with Firebase Security Rules and access controls.
  • We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active, or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

6. User Rights

  • Access the personal data we have stored about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Revoke permission for location access or other optional features at any time through app settings.

7. Third-Party Services

Hiifi Messenger uses the following third-party services to provide core functionality:

  • Firebase Authentication (for user login and account management)
  • Firebase Firestore & Storage (for storing messages and media)
  • Cloudinary (for media management and optimization, if used)

These services have their own privacy policies and follow strict security standards. However, we are not responsible for their independent practices.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be communicated in-app or via our website. Continued use of Hiifi Messenger constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
